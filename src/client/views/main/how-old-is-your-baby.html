How old is your baby squirrel?

It is important you determine the age of your baby squirrel for proper nutrition and care. Squirrels are high mainance and without the right nutrition will develop calcium deficiency a condition that can be fatal if untreated.

Age: 0-2 weeks

Characteristics

  • hairless
  • eyes closed
  • around 3 inches
  • sleeping most of the time

Age: 3-4 weeks

Characteristics

  • first signs of hair
  • lower teeth are emerging
  • eyes closed
  • sleeping most of the time

Age: 5 weeks

Characteristics

  • covered with hair
  • eyes are opening
  • upper teeth are emerging
  • starting to nibble on solid food
  • more active

Age: 6-7 weeks

Characteristics

  • thicker fuller tail
  • walking and playing
  • acting scared at humans
  • eatting solid food

Age: 8-10 weeks

Characteristics

  • bushy tail
  • extremely active
  • looking more muscular
  • gives up formula around 10 weeks

Age: 11-16 weeks old

Characteristics

  • extremely active
  • looking like an adult squirrel
  • getting ready to be released
